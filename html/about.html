No Word of God is void of power to accomplish its intent.

Regardless of how great our need is, God is greater. Whether your need is spiritual, mental,

Brief Biography: Birth to 1992

Called and confirmed to the ministry at age 11.

Began public speaking at age 14.

Attended and graduated Rhema Bible Training College in 1986.

Interned for one year at The Lord's Church, Bellflower, California.

1987-1992 - Traveled as an associate minister with Nancy Harmon Ministries.

Brief Biography: 1992 to Present

1996-2002 - Senior Pastor of Church Alive, Marietta, Georgia. 2002 to current - travels full time speaking for churches, television, seminars, conferences, and Bible schools. Has appeared on many local and national Christian television programs. Travels overseas to many countries to speak in churches, crusades, seminars, Bible schools and radio and television medias.
