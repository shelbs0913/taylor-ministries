About Jeff Taylor


"Take revival to My church." The Lord spoke to my heart after the sixth anniversary of pastoring. Resigning my church and returning to full-time, itinerant ministry was not my idea, but neither was calling me to global ministry.


Mission

Declaring and demonstrating the gospel to this generation. By balancing the Word and Spirit of God, we develop believers on eternal principles in relevant language. By making biblical principles easy to understand, we increase the effectiveness of God's promises in our own lives and, in turn, our outreach to our communities. Our passion is to see people, of all walks of life, reach their full potential in Christ.

Brief Biography Birth to 1992

  • Called and confirmed to the ministry at age 11.
  • Began public speaking at age 14.
  • Attended and graduated Rhema Bible Training College in 1986.
  • Interned for one year at The Lord's Church, Bellflower, California.
  • 1987-1992 - Traveled as an associate minister with Nancy Harmon Ministries.

Brief Biography Birth to 1992

  • 1996-2002 - Senior Pastor of Church Alive, Marietta, Georgia.
  • 2002 to current - travels full time speaking for churches, television, seminars, conferences, and
    Bible schools. Has appeared on many local and national Christian television programs.
  • Travels overseas to many countries to speak in churches, crusades, seminars, Bible schools and
    radio and television medias.
